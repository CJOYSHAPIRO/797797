797digital as frankenstein's monster

797digital was born out of a cossette lab experiment gone wrong. we are the frankenstein monster one of the largest ad agencies in the world created by being too innovative for their own good. once upon a time, the agency, a company named Telus, a tech startup and a few rogue marketers collaborated on a project so groundbreaking and disruptive that it's success had the potential to put the agency itself out of business and change the advertising industry forever. long story short: a bunch of people (dinosaurs in suits) got upset, a couple people (cool kids) got fired, and amidst the ashy dust, 797digital was born. in a nutshell, 797digital is a creative agency built ontop of a media network. on the surface, we produce content, design and manage creative campaigns like any other agency. but at our core is a powerful automated network reaching tens of millions of users a month across the blogosphere and social platforms. in other words, we have an in-house ad and content marketing platform exclusive to our clients, and we are offering it to you at zero cost. hence our ability to run entire campaigns for less than the consultancy fee at some other place. and on top of all that, we made 797digital the most transparent, client-friendly agency ever. yes, digital marketing can be this simple.
